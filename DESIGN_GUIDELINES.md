# Nuri Design Guidelines

⚠️ **WORK IN PROGRESS** - This document is under development and not yet enforced across the app.

---

## Colors

**Primary Colors**
- **Nuri Lilac** `#BEAAFF` - Primary buttons only
- **Nuri Black** `#2C232E` - Text and icons (not pure black)
- **Background** `#F0F0F0` - App background gray

---

## Primary Button (Lilac)

- Background: Lilac `#BEAAFF`
- Text: Black `#2C232E`
- Height: 54 pixels
- Shape: Fully rounded (pill)
- Optional icon on left (24×24)

---

## Secondary Button (Outlined)

- Background: Transparent
- Border: 1px Black `#2C232E`
- Text: Black `#2C232E`
- Height: 54 pixels
- Shape: Fully rounded (pill)
- Optional icon on left (24×24)

---

## Navigation Header

- Height: 44 pixels
- Side padding: 24 pixels
- Title: Inter 16pt Semibold, centered
- Left: Logo (24×24) or back arrow
- Right: Close button or small action button

**Small action button in header:**
- Text: Inter 14pt Medium
- Text color: White
- Background: Black `#2C232E`
- Padding: 12px horizontal, 6px vertical
- Shape: Rounded

---

## Tab Bar (Bottom)

- 3 tabs: Bitcoin, Card, Keys
- Icon above, label below
- Active: Black `#2C232E`
- Inactive: System gray
- Icons: ~24×24, filled style

---

## Fonts

All text uses **Inter** font family:
- Headers: Inter 16pt Semibold
- Body: Inter 16pt Regular
- Small buttons: Inter 14pt Medium
