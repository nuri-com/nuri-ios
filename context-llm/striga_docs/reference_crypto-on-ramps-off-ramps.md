# Example: Crypto On-Ramps & Off-Ramps

Whether you are an individual developer, a small business or a global company, Striga's APIs handles all the heavy lifting when it comes to building a seamless experience of buying crypto with fiat. Striga takes care of all the compliance overhead, giving you the room to focus on building your app.

## Crypto Onramps - On-chain transactions & Bank Transfers

![Onramp with USDC (ERC20)](https://files.readme.io/e958f1e-Screenshot_from_2023-06-27_15-11-55.png)

Onramp with USDC (ERC20)

Striga enables the possibility to build on-ramps, i.e. going from fiat to crypto through [On-chain transactions](https://docs.striga.com/reference/moving-money-around-1) on the **Bitcoin Network, Ethereum Network, Binance Smart Chain (BSC) Network** and/or the **Bitcoin Lightning Network**, coupled with [Bank Transfers](https://docs.striga.com/reference/enrich-account).

The onboarding flow is [fully customizable](https://docs.striga.com/reference/onboarding-flow) via the API. Simply plug in [the KYC SDK](https://docs.striga.com/reference/kyc-sdk) into your app and start onboarding your European end users, offering them dedicated deposit addresses, unique vIBANs, swaps and any functionality your application logic requires, leaving the overhead of managing compliance to us.

## Crypto Offramps - Dedicated vIBANs

![crypto-offramps](https://files.readme.io/8ad41e8-Screenshot_from_2023-06-27_15-01-57.png)

Dedicated vIBANs for SEPA payment

You can build simple fiat off-ramps by [initiating SEPA payments](https://docs.striga.com/reference/initiate-sepa-payment). Striga supports SEPA/SEPA Instant and SCT within the Eurozone, through unique, individual named vIBANs. With two API calls, you can effectively build a simple off-ramp to instantly go from crypto to fiat in the users' bank account.
